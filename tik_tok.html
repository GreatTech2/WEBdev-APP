PURPOSE

TikTok is a social media platform for creating, sharing and discovering short music video. TikTok is used by younger people as an outlet to express themselves through dancing, singing and comedy. TikTok allows the user to create 15 second video, soundtracks by music clips. It is available I around 150 countries and has over 1 billion users.

HOW IT CAN HELP YOU

While many teachers are trying to stop students from using the platform during school hours, others are embracing TikTok in the classroom in an attempt to make their assignments more interesting to kids. These teachers argue that by allowing the use of TikTok for school projects, students will retain more than they would through traditional lecture-based lessons and homework.
