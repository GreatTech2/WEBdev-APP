WHATSAPP

ORIGEN

WhatsApp was founded in 2009 by two men named Brian Acton and Jan Koum, in 2009 Koum bought an iPhone and saw the potential of an app that shows statuses next to the individual names of their users, sone he and Acton began asking Alex Fishman for advice, who then introduced the pair to Igor Solomennikov to help making an iOS application. By the 24th February 2009 WhatsApp was complete Koum named it WhatsApp because it sounded like “what’s up”.

PURPOSE

The main purpose of WhatsApp was for users to be able to send messages, images, audio or video similar to text messaging however since WhatsApp used the internet to send its messages it cost significantly less than the alternative hence why so many people use it. It can even be used on the computer or laptop and not just the phone, it also allows for group chats.

HOW TO REGASTER

How to install: In order to get whatsapp on your device you need go to the App Store or Play Store and search for it in the search bar. Then you click on the app icon and on the right-hand side there should be a little cloud if it's iPhone or a bar saving "download". You click on that and the app will be installed. How to register: Once you download the app the app will guide you through registration itself. You will be asked to confirm your country code and enter your phone number. After that you will have to verify your phone number. You will receive a message with a code which you then put into the app to clarify your phone number. Then you will be asked to add your name and a profile photo which is optional.
